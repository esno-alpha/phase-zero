Banana Bread: The driving force (along with coffee) behind esno's web development learning


Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 125g butter, melted
  • 2 teaspoons of bicarb
  • ½ cup of sugar
  • 1½ cups of gluten free flour / buckwheat flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla essence OR 2 teaspoons of cinnamon


Directions:

  1. Set oven to 180ºC
  2. Line a baking loaf tin with baking paper
  3. Warm up the butter in a saucepan, allowing it to melt over a low heat
  4. Beat the two eggs in a large mixing bowl
  5. Mash the bananas
  6. Mix in the sugar and melted butter
  7. Add the bicarb and continue mixing
  8. Tip in the flour and fold gently so there's no small specks of white
  9. Pour the bowl into the baking tin, and place in the oven for 1 hour
  10. After an hour, bring the loaf out of the oven and test with a skewer - if it comes out free of batter, it's ready
  11. Carefully lift the banana bread out of the tin (still in its baking paper) and place on a cooling rack. Wait for it to cool before cutting


Variations:

  • Lemon Blueberry | 1 whole lemon rind, finely grated, and ½ cup of blueberries, the latter pressed into the batter after it's poured into the tin
  • Cinnamon Chia Seed | 2 teaspoon of cinnamon powder and 1 tabelspoon of chia seeds
  • Choc Raspberry | ½ cup of diced dark chocolate and ½ cup of frozen raspberries, the latter pushed into the batter after it's poured into the tin
  • Pineapple Passionfruit | pulp from 1 large passionfruit or equivalent & ½ cup of fresh, diced pineapple
